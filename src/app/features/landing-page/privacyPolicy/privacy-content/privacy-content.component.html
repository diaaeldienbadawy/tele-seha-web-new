Terms And Conditions

TeleSeha places the privacy of its users — including doctors (service providers), patients (users), and website visitors — at the forefront of its priorities. This policy aims to clarify how personal and medical data is collected, used, processed, and protected, as well as the rights guaranteed to users under the applicable laws and regulations.

Article One: Data Collected

The platform collects the following types of data:

  • Registration Data: Such as full name, email address, phone number, password, account information, and more.
  • Medical Data: Including medical history, symptoms, test results, diagnostic reports, prescriptions, and other related information.
  • Service Provider Data: Like professional license number, career history, qualifications, certifications, and so on.
  • Payment Data: Such as payment receipts, electronic payment methods (with encryption and transaction protection), etc.
  • Technical Data: Including IP address, device and browser type, cookies, and similar information.

Article Two: How Data Is Used

The platform uses the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Providing medical services through the platform (such as booking appointments and conducting medical consultations).
  • Verifying the identity of users and service providers.
  • Improving user experience and developing platform services.
  • Communicating with users about appointments, updates, and notifications.
  • Fulfilling legal and regulatory obligations.
  • Research and statistical purposes after data anonymization.

Article Three: Data Sharing

  • User data is shared with service providers only to the extent necessary to provide medical consultation.
  • Data may be shared with official or judicial authorities if required by law.
  • User data is not sold or rented to any third party for marketing purposes without prior explicit consent.

The Fourth Article: Users' Rights

  • The platform's responsibility is limited to providing the technical intermediary that connects the user with the service provider.
  • The platform does not provide any direct medical consultations or treatment recommendations.
  • Full medical responsibility lies solely with the service provider.
  • The platform does not guarantee the suitability of any medical service for the user's needs; it is the user's responsibility to decide whether to receive the service and to choose the service provider.

The platform uses the collected data for the following purposes:

  • The platform uses advanced encryption protocols (SSL/HTTPS) to ensure data confidentiality during transmission.
  • Data is stored on secure servers equipped with technical and administrative protection measures.
  • Access to data is limited to authorized staff and service providers according to a strict policy.
  • Despite these measures, the platform does not guarantee absolute protection against cyber risks or attacks.

Article Six: Cookies

  • The platform uses cookies to enhance the user experience, such as saving user preferences and facilitating login.
  • Users can control cookie settings through their browser, but disabling cookies may affect some platform functions.

Article Seven: Data Retention Period

  • The platform retains user data for as long as necessary to provide the service, as long as the user account is active, or to comply with laws and regulations.
  • Data is deleted or anonymized once the purpose of use ends or after the user account is canceled.

Article Eight: Acceptance and Amendments

  • Using the platform is considered an explicit consent from users to this privacy policy.
  • The platform reserves the right to modify or update the policy at any time.
  • Continued use of the platform after the publication of amendments constitutes implicit acceptance of them.
