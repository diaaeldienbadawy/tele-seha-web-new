How Our Service Works

Our telemedicine services offer you direct access to licensed doctors— all of whom are registered with the Egyptian Ministry of Health and the Egyptian Medical Syndicate.

Telemedicine is designed to be:

  • A Direct Solution: For many health concerns, our doctors can provide a diagnosis and treatment plan right away.
  • A Support System : For cases that require a physical examination, our online consultations serve as a perfect first step or a follow-up to traditional clinic visits.
